Collection Guidelines

  1. Run AR Aging Reports regularly.
  2. Follow up with phone calls, text and email.
  3. Focus on 1–30 days past due — you have the highest chance of collection here.
  4. Make an Alternative Arrangement (AA) as soon as trouble is sensed.
  5. Come up with a realistic plan for the RP.
  6. If AA is broken, change appointment to Deb/Finish and send certified and regular postal letters.
  7. If a situation, such as job loss, has caused financial difficulties that make it challenging to continue orthodontic treatment, it may be appropriate to discuss the possibility of discontinuing (D/C) treatment with Dr. Akhavan.
Treatment does not progress on patients with a past due balance that does not have a plan for resolution. One appointment will be made for Re-tie/Hygiene check. If an AA is not made at or by that appointment, the next visit may be DEB.

Policy Rules

  1. Must be employed at the referring practice for a minimum of 6 months before courtesies become active.
  2. Only two staff members per office can be in treatment at the same time.
  3. If employment ends, monthly rate reverts to the standard (non-discounted) rate retroactive to last date of employment.
  4. Not transferable — the discount is tied exclusively to the specific referring office listed on the agreement.
  5. Referral status is based on referrals from the previous 12 months.
  6. When multiple discounts apply (e.g., sibling), only the larger discount applies.
  7. Discounts apply to non-surgical orthodontic treatment except Invisalign & Next Level Aligners (separate discounted fee).
  8. Verify employment status with the referring office before completing this form.
  9. Keep a signed copy in the patient's financial file.
