What it is NOT: Health insurance. It does not cover hospital stays, specialists, emergency care, or outside prescriptions. It works alongside or independently of any insurance you have.

401(k) Retirement Plan
Plan Name: Amir Akhavan DMD, PLLC 401(k) Plan — a Safe Harbor profit-sharing 401(k).

Eligibility: Must be 18+ years old and have completed 1 year of service (at least 1,000 hours). Entry is on the first day of the month after you meet both.
Your Eligibility Status -
Safe Harbor Match 100% on the first 3% you defer
Contribution Types Pre-tax or Roth
2025 Deferral Limit $23,500 / year · +$7,500 catch-up if 50+
Match example: If you earn $40,000/year and defer 3% ($1,200), the practice matches $1,200 on top of your contribution. The Safe Harbor Match is always 100% vested — you keep it even if you leave.
Vesting:
• Your own deferrals + Safe Harbor Match = always 100% vested
• Profit Sharing (discretionary, if declared in a year): graded — 0% (<1 yr), 25% (1 yr), 50% (2 yr), 75% (3 yr), 100% (4+ yr)

Loans: Available for any purpose. $1,000 minimum; maximum is the lesser of $50,000 or 50% of your vested balance. One outstanding loan at a time. Prime rate interest.

Withdrawals: Available at age 59½, Normal Retirement (65), hardship, disability, or termination. Early withdrawal under 59½ typically incurs a 10% penalty on the taxable portion.

Rollovers: Accepts rollovers from 401(k), 403(b), 457(b), and Traditional IRA plans.

Plan Trustee: Ascensus Trust Company · (701) 234-0207
Plan Administrator: Amir Akhavan DMD, PLLC · (352) 332-7466

To enroll: Speak with the Office Coordinator or Dr. Akhavan once eligible.
