How Accruals Work

Sick Leave: Full-time employees earn 2.83 hours per month worked, after completing orientation. Unused sick time is paid out by December 31 each year.

Vacation: Accrual rate depends on years of continuous service:

After orientation: 2.83 hrs/month (up to 34 hrs/year)
After 2 years: 5.67 hrs/month (up to 68 hrs/year)
After 5 years: 8.50 hrs/month (up to 102 hrs/year)

Vacation must be used within the calendar year it is earned. Unused vacation is paid out by December 31.

401(k) Retirement: After 1 full year of service, you are eligible for our retirement plan with a 3% employer match. See the Plan Administrator for enrollment details.
